Testimonials

“Gary is a creative and visionary artist who has a highly developed talent for seeing the relationship between form, shape and texture. He is also equally skilled and knowledgeable about building materials and construction, as well as botanicals. We first met ten years ago when our disintegrating redwood deck and expansive barren outdoor area needed some thoughtful planning. Gary worked collaboratively with us and we contracted him to draw an architectural blueprint for two new Brazilian hardwood decks, a stamped concrete patio, and an undulating rock retaining wall with a recycling water feature constructed with an antique French stone basin. The designs included new areas for planting and suggested plants. We continue to say that this was the best money we have ever spent on our home, feeling as if we are on a permanent vacation in our own backyard!”

Anne Moran & Francis Macrae

Testimonials

“Gary Marsh created a fantastic deck replacement for our woodsy and naturalistic yard. We wanted a deck that would fit with the trees that surrounded the deck area, not the standard rectangular deck. When we saw Gary’s portfolio of curved decks, we immediately knew the curves would blend perfectly with the massive curved limbs of the trees. Gary impressed us with his professionalism, and worked with our ideas to expand past the original deck area and to ‘swoop and curve’ around the trees (my words exactly). We were so impressed by the neat, tidy and organized manner of the work, and how immaculate the work area was in at the end of every day. Everyone we know has told us what a great deck we have! All thanks to Gary’s great flair for designing decks to fit the space.”

Bea, North Marin Homeowner
